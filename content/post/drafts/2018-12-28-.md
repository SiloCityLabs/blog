All you have to do is match the colors here. All 3 blacks together in a wire nut and all 3 whites. You should have a leftover wirenut.

An alternative more complicated option would be to run a 20-2 wire by pulling it through from your ballast to your primary switch. This can be quite difficult and I would recommend getting an electrician since they will have the proper tools for this.
